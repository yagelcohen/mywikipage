What is surfing anyway?

Surfing, one of the most enjoyable sports in the world.
What could be more fun than spending hours and hours in the pleasant water, catching waves and riding them to the beach?
In our opinion there are not many things in this life that equate to the feeling of catching and riding the wave.
The connection with the power of the sea, while riding a high wave and all the adrenaline flowing through your body,
is a deep connection between man and nature that only those who have experienced it can truly understand.
